Shubh Niwas Cookie Policy

Welcome to Shubh Niwas, where we aim to provide not just a home, but a lifestyle. We understand the importance of culinary activities within your home and want to ensure that your cooking experiences are both enjoyable and safe.

Our cooking policy is designed to maintain the quality of living for all residents while fostering a sense of community and respect for shared spaces.

1. Types of Cooking Allowed: Residents are permitted to engage in cooking activities that do not pose a risk to themselves or others. This includes the use of standard kitchen appliances such as stoves, ovens, microwaves, and toasters.

2. Prohibited Cooking Activities: To ensure the safety and well-being of all residents, certain cooking activities are not permitted within Shubh Niwas premises. This includes but is not limited to deep frying, grilling on open flames, and any activity that produces excessive smoke or odors.

3. Proper Ventilation: We have installed high-quality ventilation systems in all kitchens to maintain air quality and prevent the buildup of cooking odors. Residents are encouraged to use these systems while cooking to ensure a comfortable environment for everyone.

4. Fire Safety: Fire safety is of utmost importance at Shubh Niwas. Residents must adhere to all fire safety regulations and guidelines when cooking, including keeping flammable materials away from heat sources and never leaving cooking appliances unattended.

5. Respect for Shared Spaces: We encourage residents to be mindful of their neighbors when engaging in cooking activities. Excessive noise or odors that disturb other residents are not permitted. Additionally, residents should clean up after themselves and maintain a tidy cooking area.

6. Community Events: At Shubh Niwas, we believe in fostering a sense of community among residents. We regularly organize cooking events and classes where residents can learn new recipes, share culinary tips, and socialize with neighbors.

7. Guests and Visitors: Residents are welcome to have guests and visitors over for meals, provided that they adhere to the cooking policy and any additional rules set forth by Shubh Niwas management.

In conclusion, the cooking policy at Shubh Niwas is designed to promote a safe, enjoyable, and respectful environment for all residents. By following these guidelines, we can ensure that everyone can fully enjoy their home-cooked meals while maintaining harmony within our community.
